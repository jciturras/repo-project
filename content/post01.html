This is the project website for the development and testing of the Perceptions and Preferences for Meritocracy Scale.

Last update: jueves 01 abril 2021 13:32:26

Authors:

  • Juan Carlos Castillo, Departamento de Sociología, Universidad de Chile. juancastillov@uchile.cl, jc-castillo.com
  • Julio Iturra, Centro de Estudios de Conflicto y Cohesión Social - COES
  • Francisco Meneses, Departamento de Sociología, Universidad de Chile.
  • Luis Maldonado, Instituto de Sociología, Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile

In brief:

Based on a previous paper that proposed a distinction between perceptions and preferences for meritocracy and tested it with secondary survet data [@Castilloetal2019], we designed and tested a scale in a longitudinal three-wave online panel in Chile (N=1.422, final wave) through confirmatory factor analysis and measurement invariance procedures.

  • Pre-analysis: here is the description of the pre-registration of the analysis plan in the Open Science Framework, you can see it directly here:. To cite: Castillo, J. C., Iturra, J. C., Rivas, F. J. M., & Maldonado, L. (2019, November 21). Perceptions and Preferences for Meritocracy Scale (PPM-S). Retrieved from osf.io/z45y2
